3DVR Governance System

Subscription-Based Influence. Open-Source Ethics. Collective Growth.

Decentralized Governance with Subscriber Influence

3DVR is more than a tech company — it's a community-powered system. Your subscription is your vote. As a subscriber, you help guide our direction, and the more you support, the more you influence.

Free Tier: Read-only access to updates, public voting snapshots, and community involvement.
Basic Member ($20/mo): Participate in votes, access all services, join strategy discussions.
Boosted Membership (2x+ Subscriptions): Each additional $20/mo boosts your influence proportionally, treated as recurring investment.
Top Contributors: Gain access to key strategic decisions, proposal rights, and voting weight in company governance.

Security & Abuse Protection

All voting and proposal systems are transparent and open-source. Weighted influence is public, and abuse is mitigated by community oversight, time-based cooldowns, and transparency logs. Our goal is to keep 3DVR safe, fair, and collectively run.

From Subscription to Full Hardware Ecosystem

We start by offering value-packed services for $20/month: websites, apps, consulting, and unlimited tech support. As subscriptions grow, we funnel funds into open-source development of laptops, phones, and modular tools designed by and for our community.

Eventually, our vision is to offer hardware itself as part of your $20/month membership — laptops, devices, and even off-grid gear. Yes, it seems radical, but when value creation is shared with the community, it's not about big profits — it's about big impact.

Giving Back to Our Community

We don’t believe in hoarding profits. 3DVR is designed to be a regenerative system where employees and subscribers both benefit. As we grow, so do you. Whether through tech dividends, increased decision power, or access to cutting-edge tools — we’re in this together.
